Our Heritage

The Art of Jewellery

Alankar Jewellers was founded with a simple vision: to create jewellery that is not just an accessory, but a cherished memory. Since 1995, we have been a part of your celebrations, weddings, and quiet moments of joy.

Our artisans combine traditional techniques with modern aesthetics to bring you designs that are unique, elegant and timeless. We believe in purity, transparency, and building relationships that last a lifetime.

Based in Noorpur, Uttar Pradesh, every piece we craft is backed by BIS certification, ensuring you receive only the finest hallmarked jewellery.
